Safety Break

About

The Worker Ant is an application that lets you organize your managed stretch break from the desk. Worker Ant helps you decrease the chances of work-related physical/mental stress and RSI injuries. Having short and regular stretch breaks not only you can increase your productivity it can also help you to step back and reorganize your thoughts. By using Worker Ant it's easier to decrease injuries at your office you can Be comfortable and enjoy your work more than ever.

Worker Ant is fully custom program. By clicking on the gear icon on the top right corner, you can open up the settings window. In the setting window, you can easily optimize the options to suit your workflow. (e.g. by changing the number of rounds, you can plan your upcoming hours.)

Worker Ant is developed with C# the successor to Safety break. Worker Ant is in development app that is ready to be used for personal use. The development of this project will be continued.
